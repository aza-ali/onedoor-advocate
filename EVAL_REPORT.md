## Why two numbers (honesty over a single flattering figure)

- **B1(a) is consistency, not accuracy.** Two personas are scored against PolicyEngine-US (an
  independent rules-as-code implementation). We compare **verdicts** (eligible vs. not), not
  dollars, because PolicyEngine models calendar-2026 H.R.1 allotment phase-downs that are not in
  the statutory FY2026 tables our engine cites. Verdicts agree 100%; we do not claim dollar
  agreement we did not earn.
- **B2 is the gate.** The 14 INDEPENDENT cases carry expected verdicts and dollar amounts
  hand-worked from the cited statute (7 CFR 273.9 / 273.10, the FY2026 USDA COLA tables, CA BBCE).
  If the engine had an implementation bug, it would diverge from the hand-worked answer. It does
  not: precision 100%, mean dollar error $0.
